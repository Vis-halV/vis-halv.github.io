key takeaways

This project was full of exploration and hands-on learning. Here's the essence of what I took away!

UI IS MY JAM, BACKEND NOT SO MUCH

I loved building the visual side, but backend was tricky. I got a milestone done—storing contact messages in Firebase—but the story editor remained unfinished. Solo work made it a steep learning curve!

PERKS OF BEING NEW TO THE DOMAIN

Jumping in fresh let me focus on crafting a UI that's visually appealing and psychologically solid, without old habits clouding my approach.
